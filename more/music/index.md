<p class="Section">1. A Lifelong Rhythm</p>

<p class="BodyText">
Since I was a child, music has been a constant companion. I have always enjoyed feeling the rhythm and letting it guide my emotions. Over the years, music has evolved from a simple pleasure to a deep form of personal expression.
</p>

<div class="sport-row">
  <img src="/assets/images/sports/djj.jpg" alt="Futsal de niño" class="sport-avatar">
<p class="BodyText">
At the age of 15, I began experimenting with mixing tracks, captivated by the art of blending sounds and creating seamless transitions. This passion soon led me to produce my own music, where I discovered the joy of crafting melodies and textures from scratch.
</p>
</div>

<p class="Section">2. Influences & Style</p>

<p class="BodyText">
My greatest musical reference has always been and will always be <strong>Avicii</strong>. His ability to blend emotional melodies with energetic beats continues to inspire me. However, the genres where I feel most comfortable are <strong>ambient</strong> and <strong>minimal</strong> music. I find peace in the simplicity of ambient textures and the hypnotic repetition of minimal rhythms.
</p>

<p class="BodyText">
Whenever I have free time, I try to create something new. Whether it is a full track or just a few minutes of ambient soundscapes, the process of creating music is a form of meditation and discovery for me.
</p>
